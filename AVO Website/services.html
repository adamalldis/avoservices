Electro Static Disinfecting

Electrostatic spraying provides significantly better coverage of surfaces, as opposed to a typical directional sprayer. Electrically charged liquids create a fine mist through the air which has a 360° wrap around effect. This allows the disinfectant to reach especially tight spaces that conventional disinfecting methods would otherwise miss. These charged droplets spread evenly and reduce dripping, making for a much neater application and easier clean up. It also fights odours and mold, and germs are completely eradicated upon contact with the disinfectant.
