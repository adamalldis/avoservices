Seasonal Planters

AVO's seasonal planters add that extra touch of luxury to your home's entrance or backyard. Available for all four seasons, our planters will bring your landscape to life.

Typically, we'll use your existing containers to create your planters but if you'd like new containers, AVO Property Services can help you choose the perfect fit for your space.

Spring Planters

Spring planters are the perfect way to usher in the warmer weather and add a splash of colour to your property. We'll use plants that thrive in rainy conditions and last long into the season. Beware of ordering too early as unexpected frost can reduce the longevity of the plants.

Summer Planters

Our summer planters use the heartiest plants. With adequate watering these planters can last the entire summer season. They thrive in hot, full-sun locations while at the same time bringing beautiful colour to your entranceway or backyard oasis.

Autumn Planters

The transition from summer to fall is one of the most colourful times of the year. Our autumn planters reflect that with their beauty, using mums and other fall-hearty plants to amplify your outside decor. A regular watering schedule will keep these planters healthy and vibrant in the weeks leading up to winter.

Winter Planters

Just because it's cold outside doesn't mean your property can't still look its best. Our winter planters use cold-resistant plants including but not limited to evergreen, boxwoods, and more. Some winter plants still require occassional watering which our experts will happily explain to you.
